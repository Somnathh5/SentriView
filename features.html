Crowd monitoring

Crowd monitoring involves tracking and analyzing the movement and density of people in a specific area to ensure safety and optimize resource management. Advanced technologies such as surveillance cameras and data analytics are often used to detect potential issues and improve crowd control.

safety alert

Our system detects when individuals cross safety lines at metro stations, triggering immediate alerts to prevent accidents and ensure passenger safety. Real-time monitoring helps manage crowd flow and enforce safety regulations effectively.

fall detection

Our fall detection system promptly identifies when an individual falls, automatically sending alerts to emergency services. This technology ensures rapid response and enhances safety for vulnerable individuals.

abandoned luggage

Our abandoned baggage detection system scans for unattended items in real-time, triggering alerts to prevent potential security threats. This proactive measure enhances safety and ensures quick intervention.
